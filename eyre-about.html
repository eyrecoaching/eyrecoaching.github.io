About

Not a clinical background. An operational one. The difference matters.

Where this started
Not a moment.
Years.

EYRE did not begin with an insight. It began with the accumulated cost of sustained stress across decades — AFP Protective Services, counter-terrorism, private security contracting in conflict zones, national operations management. High-functioning, high-stakes, high-cost. The nervous system that served me in those environments did not switch off when the roles did.

Regulation was not something I came to through curiosity. I tracked it down because I needed it — and because the standard answers did not work for the way I was built or the life I had lived. Mindfulness. Rest. The advice people give when they have not spent years in activation.

What worked was physiological. Precise. Evidence-based. The breath, the vagus nerve, the autonomic nervous system — tools that operate below the level of thinking and willpower. EYRE is built from that search — in quiet spaces, in remote country, in the places where the noise stops and the real state of the system becomes impossible to ignore.

Why breathwork. Why this.
The breath is the only part of the autonomic nervous system you can control directly.

Everything else — heart rate, cortisol, the threat response — operates below conscious access. The breath sits at the intersection. Change the breath deliberately and you change the physiology underneath it. Not metaphorically. Mechanically.

That is not a wellness claim. It is how the vagus nerve works. It is what the research on HRV, resonance frequency, and parasympathetic activation consistently shows. EYRE is built on that evidence — not on ideology, not on tradition, not on what sounds compelling in a brochure.

The tools are simple. The application is precise. The outcomes are real and they are yours to keep.
