Privacy Policy

Effective April 2026
1
Who We Are

EYRE Coaching is a trading name of Arkana Life, an ABN-registered sole trader operating in South Australia, Australia. This privacy policy explains how EYRE Coaching collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

If you have questions about this policy or how your information is handled, contact us at chris@eyrecoaching.com.

2
What Information We Collect

We may collect the following personal information when you interact with EYRE Coaching:

—
Your name and email address when you submit an enquiry, join a waitlist, or express interest through our website.
—
Any information you voluntarily provide in a message or contact form.
—
Booking and scheduling information when you use Calendly to book a discovery call or session.
—
Payment information when you purchase a service, processed through Stripe. EYRE Coaching does not store card details — these are held by Stripe under their own privacy policy.

Our self-regulation tools do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No account is required to use them and no usage data is retained.

3
How We Use Your Information

We use personal information only for the purposes for which it was provided:

—
To respond to enquiries and expressions of interest.
—
To notify waitlist members when new programmes or services become available.
—
To manage bookings and scheduled sessions.
—
To process payments for services.

We do not use your personal information for unsolicited marketing. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their own commercial purposes.

4
Third Party Services

EYRE Coaching uses the following third party services which may collect and process personal information under their own privacy policies:

—
Calendly — used for booking discovery calls and sessions. When you book via Calendly, your information is collected and stored by Calendly Inc. under their privacy policy at calendly.com/privacy.
—
Stripe — used for payment processing. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.
—
Squarespace — this website is hosted on Squarespace. Squarespace may collect analytics data in accordance with their privacy policy at squarespace.com/privacy.
5
How We Store and Protect Your Information

Personal information submitted via contact forms is received by email and stored in our Google Workspace account (chris@eyrecoaching.com), which is secured by Google's enterprise privacy and security standards.

We take reasonable steps to protect personal information from misuse, interference, loss, unauthorised access, modification, or disclosure. We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purpose for which it was collected.

6
Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Principles you have the right to:

—
Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
—
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
—
Request deletion of your personal information, subject to any legal obligations to retain it.
—
Withdraw consent to being contacted for future communications.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at chris@eyrecoaching.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7
Cookies and Analytics

This website may use cookies and analytics tools provided by Squarespace to understand how visitors use the site. These cookies do not identify you personally. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

EYRE Coaching does not use advertising cookies or tracking pixels for third party advertising purposes.

8
Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page will reflect the date of the most recent revision. Continued use of EYRE Coaching services following any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

9
Complaints

If you believe we have handled your personal information in a way that does not comply with the Australian Privacy Principles, please contact us at chris@eyrecoaching.com. We will investigate and respond within 30 days.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.
